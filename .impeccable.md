# Impeccable — Project Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Owners and sole directors of small / dormant UK limited companies — typically **not accountants**. They use DormantFile to file statutory annual accounts (Companies House) and CT600 (HMRC) cheaply, without paying an accountant. Usage is **episodic and deadline-driven**, not daily: a user shows up because something is due (or overdue), needs to understand exactly what they owe and by when, files it, and wants unambiguous confirmation it's done. Frequently on a phone, often anxious about penalties and getting a legal filing wrong.

### Brand Personality
Trustworthy, plain-spoken, calm. Three words: **reassuring, exact, unfussy.** It is filing software for legal obligations — it must feel like it knows the rules and won't let you make a mistake, without lecturing. No hype, no marketing gloss inside the app.

### Aesthetic Direction
Existing dark theme is **fixed** (slate/navy tokens in `globals.css`, `.dark` overrides) — colour scheme changes are out of scope by directive. Visual tone: closer to a **bank statement / filing ledger** than a SaaS dashboard. Quiet, dense-but-legible, hierarchy through type and space rather than chrome. Anti-reference: the generic "grid of identical shadowed cards, each card containing a tinted inner card" admin-template look.

### Design Principles
1. **The content is a dated worklist, not a gallery.** Filing periods are rows in a ledger, not cards in a grid. Don't wrap a single row in card chrome.
2. **One surface, not nested surfaces.** No card-in-card. At most one container depth on any screen; prefer hairline dividers and spacing over stacked backgrounds.
3. **Hierarchy by emphasis, not by box.** The earliest outstanding period and its one primary action are the focal point; everything else recedes via weight/size/colour-already-in-token, not extra borders.
4. **Mobile is the primary case, not a shrink.** Rows reflow to stacked blocks with a full-width primary action; nothing critical is hidden or amputated.
5. **Work within existing functionality and tokens.** Recompose; never invent features or change the palette.
