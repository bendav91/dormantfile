Every plan includes both filings — dormant accounts to Companies House and nil CT600 returns to HMRC — for any outstanding period. Plans are based on how many companies you manage, not how many filings you make. Your HMRC Gateway credentials are used once at the point of submission and never stored.

## Billing questions

### Can I try before I commit?

You have a 14-day cooling-off period after subscribing. If you cancel within those 14 days and haven't submitted a filing, we issue a full refund. See our [refund and cancellation policy](/refund) for full details.

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel via the billing portal in your account settings. Your subscription stays active until the end of your current billing period. We do not offer refunds for partial periods after the 14-day cooling-off window.

### Can I upgrade or downgrade?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. Both are handled from your account settings.

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards via Stripe. We don't currently accept bank transfers or direct debits.

### Do you offer refunds?

During the first 14 days you can cancel for a full refund, provided no filing has been submitted. After that, we don't refund partial billing periods — but your access continues until the period ends. Full details are in our [refund and cancellation policy](/refund).
