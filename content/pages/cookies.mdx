Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site work properly and can provide information to the site owner. You can read more about cookies in the [ICO's guidance on cookies](https://ico.org.uk/for-organisations/direct-marketing-and-privacy-and-electronic-communications/guide-to-pecr/cookies-and-similar-technologies/).

This page explains which cookies DormantFile sets, why we use them, and how you can control your preferences.

## Essential cookies

These cookies are required for the site to function. They cannot be switched off.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration | Type |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `next-auth.session-token` | Keeps you logged in | Session (deleted on sign-out or browser close) | Essential |
| `next-auth.csrf-token` | Prevents cross-site request forgery | Session | Essential |
| `next-auth.callback-url` | Remembers where to redirect you after login | Session | Essential |

## Cookie consent

Your consent preference (accept or decline) is stored in your browser's local storage rather than in a cookie. This lets us remember your choice without setting an additional cookie.

## Analytics cookies (optional, consent required)

We use Google Analytics to understand how people use DormantFile so we can improve the service.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration | Type |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `_ga` | Distinguishes unique visitors (Google Analytics) | 2 years | Analytics |
| `_ga_*` | Maintains session state (Google Analytics) | 2 years | Analytics |

Analytics cookies are **only set if you click "Accept"** on our cookie consent banner. If you decline, no analytics cookies are placed and no data is sent to Google. Google Analytics data is used in aggregate to see which pages are visited and how the service is used. We do not use it to identify individual users.

## Advertising cookies

We do not use any advertising or third-party tracking cookies. We do not sell or share your data with advertisers.

## How to manage your preferences

You can change your cookie preference at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site. The consent banner will reappear on your next visit, and you can make a new choice.

You can also block or delete cookies through your browser settings. Be aware that blocking essential cookies may prevent you from signing in.

For more information about how we handle your data, see our [privacy policy](/privacy). For general guidance on cookies, visit the [ICO's cookie guidance](https://ico.org.uk/for-organisations/direct-marketing-and-privacy-and-electronic-communications/guide-to-pecr/cookies-and-similar-technologies/).
