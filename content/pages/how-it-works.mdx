<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up with your email address and set a password. It takes about 30 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your company">
    Enter your company registration number and we look up the name from Companies House
    automatically. Then add your UTR (Unique Taxpayer Reference) and your accounting period dates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your plan">
    Pick the plan that fits. Basic is £19/year for one company, Multiple is £39/year for up to 10,
    and Agent is £49/year for up to 100.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get deadline reminders">
    We calculate your filing deadlines automatically — nine months after your accounting reference
    date for accounts, twelve months for CT600. You get email reminders at 90, 30, 14, 7, 3, and 1
    day before each deadline.
  </Step>
  <Step title="File your accounts">
    Submit your dormant accounts to Companies House. Enter your authentication code and we handle
    the rest via the Companies House software filing API.
  </Step>
  <Step title="File your CT600">
    Submit a nil CT600 to HMRC. Enter your Government Gateway credentials — they are used once and
    never stored. We file directly via HMRC's GovTalk API.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get confirmation">
    Once your filing is accepted, we show confirmation in your dashboard and send you an email.
    Filing records are stored so you always have a history.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout>
  **Behind on your filings?** DormantFile shows all outstanding periods for your company. File them
  one at a time, starting from the oldest, and your company will be brought up to date.
</Callout>

<Callout>
  **Not registered for Corporation Tax?** No problem. Accounts and CT600 are independent filings —
  you can file one without the other. Many dormant companies only need to file annual accounts with
  Companies House. Read our guide on [whether you need a
  CT600](/guides/do-i-need-ct600-dormant-company) for more detail.
</Callout>
